## Objection 1: "Đắt quá / Nhiều kênh free hơn"

**Tâm lý:** Chưa thấy value. So sánh với free = chưa hiểu khác biệt.

**Counter:**
"Free channels: 1 guy, 1 chart, 'buy here sell there.' No system behind it. Here: 5 gates + 12 agents verify every signal. You're not paying for signals — you're paying for the system that produced 94% WR and TOP 1 FTMO. 1 bad trade costs more than 1 month of VIP. The math is simple."
